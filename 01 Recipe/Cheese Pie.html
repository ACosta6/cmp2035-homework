Making a Cheese Pie

by Ikea
This recipe serves 6.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cup shredded OST PRAST (semi hard cheese)
  • 3 tbsp fresh chopped dill
  • 1 1/4 cup wheatflour
  • 4 eggs
  • 10 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 5 oz heavy cream
  • 7 tbsp chopped chives
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
    •

    Steps to make the Cheese Pie:

    1. Mix butter and wheatflour.
    2. Add water and quickly work into a dough.
    3. Leave to cool for 30 minutes.
    4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheight
    5. Roll out the dough to fit the size of your pie dish, preferably 8-9" in diameter.
    6. Fold the dough about 1/2" in around the edge so that it does not slide down during the baking.
    7. To let the steam out during baking, poke holes in the bottom of the dough using a fork.
    8. Pre-bake the dough: 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes
    9. While that is prebaking, mix egg cheese, cream, chopped chives, and dill.
    10. Add salt and pepper
    11. Once dough is pre-baked, pour the filling in the pie dish and bake for another 25-30 minutes.

    Not enough time to cook? Try PAJ OST cheese pie instead!
